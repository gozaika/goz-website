# Restaurant Onboarding Product Notes

Slice 2 establishes the P0 onboarding promise for restaurants:

- Restaurant owner login through Zayka Pro.
- Idempotent restaurant onboarding creation.
- Restaurant basics, pickup instructions, public profile headline/story.
- Compliance details for FSSAI, GSTIN, PAN, and masked payout account fields.
- Private document upload for required FSSAI/KYC artifacts.
- Admin review for documents and compliance.
- Activation only after compliance approval and onboarding task completion/waiver.

Out of scope for Slice 2:

- BAM Bag templates and drops.
- Inventory, orders, payments, pickup verification.
- Finance settlement and invoice workflows.
- Analytics and Zayka Pro insights.
- OCR or external KYC provider integrations.
